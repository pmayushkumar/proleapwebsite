Frequently Asked Questions

Who is ProLeap for?

For anyone who believes "soft skills" are slowing down their professional growth!

Typically for Senior Developers, Senior Product Managers, Senior Designers or other senior professionals who know that the next level of growth depends on non-functional skills like alignment, assertiveness, strategic presence, judgement, influence and others.

How does ProLeap work?

It's a simple four-step process:

  1. Provide speaker and microphone access to your laptop
  2. Connect your work calendar
  3. Tell us about your professional context, aspirations, and goals.
  4. Sit back and watch the magic unfold
    • Coach analyzes your conversations against world-class frameworks to identify behavioral patterns and "missed moments."
    • After every meeting, you'll receive high-impact insights
    • Every week, you will receive a digest that connects the dots on your blind spots
    • Providing a clear roadmap to improve

How soon can I expect to see measurable growth in my professional impact?

Most users identify a key communication "blind spot" within their first three meetings. By using our Progress Tracker and Executive Mirror, you can quantify improvements in areas like active listening and executive presence over a 30-to-90-day period.

Can I personalize the type of coaching I receive?

ProLeap is as personalized as you want it to be. You can provide specific context on your stakeholders' personalities, your company culture, and your own personal aspirations or constraints. The more context you share, the more the AI's intelligence evolves to provide hyper-relevant feedback over time. Context can be added & edited within user settings.

Why do you need speaker, microphone, calendar access?

ProLeap acts as an ambient partner that listens via your laptop's speaker and microphone. No external bots join your calls, allowing your coach to run seamlessly in the background while giving you the choice to inform participants if you wish.

How does ProLeap keep my information safe?

Your meeting data, transcripts, and coaching advice are stored locally on your own system, ensuring you maintain full ownership. While we process data through LLM models to power the product's insights, we do not store that information on our servers.

Only you have access to your data; no one else. Effective coaching requires the space to be vulnerable, and we ensure that your growth journey stays strictly between you and your AI coach.

Read our privacy policy for more details: proleap.ai/privacy

Who is the coach behind ProLeap?

ProLeap is grounded on coaching styles & frameworks of 50+ world-class coaches like Kim Scott, Simon Sinek, Tony Robbins, Donna Carlin, Carl Jung and more. Each coach has their own style & expertise, and all their knowledge comes together to help you in your unique situation.

Does it cost anything to use the app?

ProLeap is currently free as we build the future of AI coaching. We are actively looking for alpha users to join our early cohort, use the tool, and provide the feedback that will shape our product roadmap.

By when can I expect to receive access to the app?

We are rolling out access to our waitlist in small batches to ensure a high-quality experience for every early adopter. Once you sign up, you can typically expect an invitation to join our alpha testing phase within 2–4 weeks.

What if I need help or have questions?

Please send an email to support@proleap.ai, and expect a response within 2-3 working days.
