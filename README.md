## What is ProLeap?

ProLeap is an AI coaching platform that analyzes your real meetings and delivers actionable leadership feedback — no self-reporting, no scheduling sessions with a coach, no guesswork.

It works silently in the background during your meetings, then provides personalized coaching insights based on what actually happened.

## How It Works

1. **Record** — ProLeap joins your meetings (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams) and captures the conversation
2. **Analyze** — AI analyzes the transcript through multiple coaching lenses (communication, leadership, collaboration, influence)
3. **Coach** — You receive a detailed coaching report with specific, evidence-based feedback tied to real moments in your meeting
4. **Grow** — Track your progress over time across focus areas, with an AI coach you can chat with anytime

## Key Features

- **Automated Meeting Analysis** — No manual input needed. Just have your meetings and get coached.
- **Evidence-Based Feedback** — Every insight is backed by a specific quote and timestamp from your meeting.
- **Multiple Coaching Lenses** — Communication clarity, leadership presence, strategic thinking, stakeholder management, and more.
- **Chat with Your Coach** — Ask follow-up questions, prep for upcoming meetings, or reflect on patterns.
- **Progress Tracking** — See how you're improving across focus areas over weeks and months.
- **Interview Coaching** — Dedicated mode for job interview preparation and post-interview analysis.
- **Privacy-First** — Your data stays on your device. Transcripts are processed locally.

## Who Is It For?

- **Engineering Managers** navigating 1:1s, standups, and cross-functional meetings
- **Product Managers** running stakeholder reviews, sprint planning, and customer calls
- **Sales Leaders** refining their pitch, discovery calls, and negotiation
- **Anyone preparing for job interviews** who wants real, evidence-based feedback
