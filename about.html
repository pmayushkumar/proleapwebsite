About us

We built ProLeap because high-quality, personalized coaching shouldn't be reserved for the C-suite.

High potential senior ICs start receiving feedback like "Work on your executive presence" or "You need to be more strategic" or "increase your visibility". That feedback is not only ambiguous but frustrating; to be told what to fix without being shown how to do it in the flow of your actual work.

This gap leads to cycles of anxiety and untapped potential. ProLeap was built to bridge that gap.

Professional growth shouldn't be a mystery. You don't lack talent. You lack a mirror.

ProLeap is an ambient partner that understands your specific context; your stakeholders, your company culture, and your unique aspirations. It is designed to be the coach we always wanted: one that observes your real-time performance and knows exactly when you're losing the audience, when you're being too directive, or when you've finally hit your stride.

Our "Privacy-First" DNA

Your vulnerability is a feature, not a data point.

Coaching requires trust. ProLeap is built with a "Privacy-First, Local-First" DNA. Your data stays on your machine. Your "missed moments" stay between you and your coach.
